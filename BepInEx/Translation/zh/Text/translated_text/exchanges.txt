"The wheezing, smoking, frequently burning, always energetic engine yard for the eccentric engineers of Portsmouth House."=不停的轰鸣声、冒着烟、炽热的温度、总是精力充沛的朴茨茅斯机车厂里古怪工程师们的机房。
Requisitions=征购处
"The Weary Quartermaster is willing to write off some of Portsmouth House's output, for a price."=疲倦的军需官愿意“报废”朴茨茅斯的一些产品——但要付出一些代价。
Sky Barnet=天空巴尼特
The gateway to the Blue Kingdom; held by London's Embassy and coveted by devils.=通往蓝色王国的大门；由伦敦的大使馆控制，并为魔鬼所觊觎。
The Last Autumnal Slope=最后的秋日之坡
"The Ambassador's sister is entrusted with the provisioning of armaments and equipment to visiting captains. There was, apparently, no one better suited to the task."=大使的妹妹负责为来访的车长提供军用设备和生活供给。显然，没有人比她更适合这项任务。
Pilgrim's Progress=天路历程
"A beguilingly luxuriant engine-yard: where chassis gleam like starlight and both engines and engineers are coiffed, polished and meticulously upholstered. (If your locomotive has suffered more than 10 damage, its sale value will be reduced by 500 sovereigns.)"=一个令人眼花缭乱的豪华机车厂：那里的机车底盘像星光一样闪闪发光，机师和引擎都被抛光、打磨、和精心装饰过。（如果你的机车损坏超过10点，其销售价值将减少500金镑）。
The Turret=炮塔
A desperate attempt by London's Embassy to supplement funds. The Ambassador's smile is manic as she invites visiting captains to peruse the Embassy's wares.=伦敦大使馆为补充资金而进行的一次绝望的尝试。大使的笑容很兴奋，她邀请来访的车长们参观大使馆的商品。
The Forge of Souls=灵魂铸炉
A place that once had great purpose; no longer.=这里曾经有着崇高的目的;曾经。
The Venturer's Request=冒险家的要求
"One of the first Londoners to cross the Avid Horizon; the Venturer skulks by the Forge, collecting shards of Navaratine. He parts, reluctantly, with the least of these."=作为第一批穿越渴求之地平线的沦敦人之一，冒险家在铸炉旁兜兜转转，收集曩縛咤宝石的碎片。他很不情愿地丢弃了其中最小的那一块。
The White Well=白井
"A palace of statues and spiders; a fortress on the edge of a well. Here, souls are judged and prisoners are held."=一个由雕像和蜘蛛组成的宫殿；一个位于井边的堡垒。在这里，灵魂被审判，囚犯被关押。
The RagandBone=拉格朗德的骨头
A market for the cast-offs of the prisoners held in the Well. The Graven who run the stalls regard you with alternately bafflement and contempt.=这是被关在井里的囚犯的弃物的市场。经营摊位的格雷文人以莫名其妙和蔑视的眼光看待你。
Death's Door=死亡之门
The edge of eternity; the precipice of mortality; the final horizon.=永恒的边界；死亡的悬崖；最后的地平线。
Trinity's Hoarding=三位一体的秘藏处
"A masked, silent trio of spirits trade in souls here. Their motivations are obscure and they are forbidden from passing on. Their eye for quality, however, is impeccable."=三个戴着面具、沉默不语的幽魂在这里进行灵魂交易。他们的交易动机无人得知，他们被禁止欺骗。然而，他们对灵魂质量的鉴别是无可挑剔的。
The Gentlemen=绅士
smuggling=走私
"The Gentlemen are the skies' most notorious smugglers, an extensive gang unafraid of murder or the hangman. Their primary export is starshine: waters steeped in the far-travelled light of stars that once shone upon old earth."=绅士们是天空中最臭名昭著的走私者，是一个不怕谋杀或绞刑的庞大团伙。他们的主要出口产品是星光酒——一种浸泡着曾经照耀过古老地球的遥远星光的液体。
Wit & Vinegar=尖酸&狡黠
"A front for a more successful smuggling concern, the Wit & Vinegar Lumber Company has interests across Albion and the Reach. Its chief contraband is illicit literature, largely secured from malcontents in the University's English Department. "=作为一个更成功的走私公司的幌子，尖酸&狡黠木材公司的利益遍布阿尔比恩和外野。它的主要走私品是非法文学，基本都是从大学英语系的不满者那里获得的。
